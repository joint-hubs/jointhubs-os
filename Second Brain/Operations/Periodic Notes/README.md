| Folder | Format | Purpose |
|--------|--------|---------|

| `Weekly/` | `YYYY-Www.md` | Weekly reviews and planning |
| `Monthly/` | `YYYY-MM.md` | Monthly reviews |
| `Quarterly/` | `YYYY-Qq.md` | Quarterly reviews |
